# Operations

## Purpose

Internal operating system for Axiom AI Systems.

## What Belongs Here

- Daily prospecting checklist
- Outreach tracking process
- Follow-up rhythm
- Offer notes
- Client onboarding checklist
- Delivery checklist
- Weekly review process

## Operating Rhythm

Daily:

- Review 10 businesses
- Save 3-5 qualified prospects
- Send 5 targeted messages
- Update follow-up dates

Weekly:

- Review pipeline
- Improve message templates
- Refine ICP notes
- Identify offer objections
- Decide next build priority
