# Client Intake

## Purpose

A lightweight intake process for local service businesses interested in an AI Systems Audit.

## Goals

- Capture business context
- Identify repetitive admin work
- Understand lead sources and follow-up process
- Prepare for a useful audit conversation

## Intake Topics

- Business name and services
- Main customer questions
- Quote or booking workflow
- Current tools
- Follow-up and reminder process
- Review request process
- Biggest manual bottleneck
